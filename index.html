Hi, I'm Lys.

My full name is Iliass Hajji, I was born and raised in Morocco, and I'm a front-end web developer with a background in graphic and logo design, and I'm looking to further enhance my web development skills.

I've always been fascinated with computers and I always wanted to dive deep in how things like the internet and computers work.

In my journey learning web development, I learned that there is nothing magical about computers and the internet, only a meticulous interplay of logical procedures, complex communication between hardware and software, translating code into the binary language of 1s and 0s, controlling the amount of light pixels must emit using these bits, and displaying the information you request through these pixels.

I believe that nothing is beyond the grasp of learning if you break each difficult concept into its smallest, understandable and comprehensible element. This way of thinking served as guideline principle for me to learn the things I know, and will always do for the things I want to learn.

By breaking down complex ideas into manageable fragments, I've found that even the most difficult concepts that I encountered and that previously I thought were impossible, became accessible and understandable.

Currently, I maintain my personal website, and I also build and maintain other study projects that you can find in my GitHub account.

I'm always down to collaborate when I'm free. Contact me if you have any questions.
